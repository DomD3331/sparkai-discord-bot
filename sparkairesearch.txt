Products:

SmartTakeoff:
SmartTakeoff is a contractor-focused construction operating system developed by SparkAIResearch.

Purpose:
Help contractors organize information, estimate projects, manage scope, review materials, and improve operational efficiency.

Important Doctrine:

* SmartTakeoff is not an authority.
* SmartTakeoff does not verify code compliance.
* SmartTakeoff does not certify pricing.
* SmartTakeoff does not guarantee scope completeness.
* SmartTakeoff assists contractors by organizing, drafting, tracing, and presenting information.
* Licensed contractors remain responsible for final decisions.
